How to start?

  1. Select the type of group you are playing with.
  2. Take turns going around the room, choosing a category (column) and level (row).
  3. If you complete the card, give the level’s corresponding drink to whomever you want.
  4. If you don’t want to do the card then drink the corresponding amount of the level chosen.
  5. Play responsibly and have fun!
